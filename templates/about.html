About Me



My name is Ricards Graudins and I created this website as a project for Data Represenation and Querying module
as part of my undergraduate course Software Developement.

I'v been playing League of Legends since season 1 and took a year off college to play league competively at the highest level and
got to face some of the most talented players throughout the year. Through this experience I became motivated to create a website
that would provide insight on the various play styles based on my favorite position that is top lane.

Play styles are a massive part of League of Legends and champions that can preform multiple roles/ excel at one role have
a huge impact on the outcome of the game. Thus I created multiple guides for various champions which are no where near finished
and will be updated as the game changes drastically every so often. At the moment the guides contain fundamental basics
of playing a champion. However these basics are not to be discarded easily as mastering them will lead to better preformance
and are the stepping stones of carrying out advanced mechanics and climbing the ranked ladder.

Although these guides provide a lot of insight on how to play your champion, it is important to note that your skill level
won't skyrocket over 9000 from simply reading one of the guides. It is vital to understand all of the champions to be able
to adjust your play style in any situation and the most important rule of all is: practise makes perfect!

If you find yourself in "Elo Hell" or struggling to improve due to a toxic team, follow these 3 simple rules:

1. Don't Tilt
2. Focus only on your individual preformance
3. Learn from your mistakes

If you wish to submit a guide or provide insight on a champion you can contact me at zionmlg@gmail.com or send me
a private message in game on the EUW server.

Good luck and have fun!
